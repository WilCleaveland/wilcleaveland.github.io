Hey there!

Thanks for stopping by. Let me tell you a little about what I do.

I love writing copy.

Messages weild boundless power. You live in a world constructed entirely of the meaning you and the people around you create, and all that meaning can only be channeled one way: the messages we build in our heads and deliver to one another each and every day.

The tricky part is getting those messages right.

Thankfully, it doesn't have to be rocket science. A little research, empathy, and insight to the communicative circuitry that's been hardwired into our brains for centuries will give you a pretty good start.

The exciting part is when you realize that strategic communication is the ultimate method for affecting change. Connecting great products with hungry consumers, transforming bystanders into advocates, and compelling chronic skimmers to stop and read the fine print - it all hinges on the message!

So let me rephrase here: I love writing copy building messages that make things happen.

Clicks, opt-ins, purchases, comments, likes, shares - whatever the metric may be, I like to see needles jump and graphs turn into hockey sticks. It takes work, and I don't get it right every time, but that's what makes it fun.

I'm guessing you might feel the same way. If you do, send me a message and we'll talk.

Cheers,
